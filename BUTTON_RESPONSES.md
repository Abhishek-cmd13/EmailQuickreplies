## How It Works

1. **First Click:** Borrower receives personalized response based on their choice
2. **Remaining Buttons:** Also receives the other 3 buttons to continue the flow
3. **Subsequent Clicks:** Each click generates a different response
4. **Final Click:** Completion message when all buttons are selected
